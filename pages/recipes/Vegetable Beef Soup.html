Ingredients

1 lb of ground beef
2 lb bag of frozen mixed veggies (corn, green beans, carrots)
4 cups of beef broth (I used reduced sodium)
1 small can of tomato paste
2-3 celery stalks
2 big carrots
1/2 sweet onion chopped
1/3 - 1/2 cup Barley
Seasonings: salt, pepper, garlic powder, Italian seasoning
Optional: 1/2 cabbage, garlic salt

Directions

Directions:
- Put ground beef into pot and fill with water until covered
- While ground beef is cooking, chop onion, add onion, celery, carrots, seasonings: ~1 TB of salt, 1 tsp pepper, garlic powder, Italian
- As beef browns, add more water to cover ingredients and simmer until vegetables are soft
- After vegetables are soft, take celery and carrots out to cool (throw away if you don’t want to chop them back into soup)
- Add 2 lb bag of mixed veggies, 1 can of tomato paste, beef broth
- If all ingredients are NOT submerged, add more water
- Bring to a light boil then simmer for 1-2 hours (the longer it summers, the more flavor it has)
- Add barley and continue to simmer (same simmer note as above)
